Section 01

Introduction

ALGSoft Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") built Digi Voice as a commercial amateur radio digital voice communication application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the Digi Voice app on iOS, macOS, and watchOS devices.

Digi Voice is intended for licensed amateur radio operators. Use of this application requires a valid amateur radio license issued by your country's telecommunications authority.

⚠️ By using Digi Voice, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application.
Section 02

Digi Voice Collects No Data

🔒 Plain language: Digi Voice collects nothing. It has no backend, no database, no analytics, and no crash reporting. It is a radio protocol client — a pipe between your device and the radio network server you choose to connect to. No data ever reaches ALGSoft Inc. or any server we operate.

Your Callsign, DMR ID and Passwords Belong to Third-Party Platforms

Your callsign and DMR/NXDN ID are credentials you registered on RadioID.net, BrandMeister, TGIF, or another radio network — independently of Digi Voice. Digi Voice does not issue, register, store, or manage these credentials on its own servers.

When you enter your callsign and password in the Settings screen, that information is saved only on your local device and sent directly from your device to the third-party server you select. We never receive it.

Diagnostic Log Files

The app writes session logs to the device's Caches directory (not iCloud-synced). Logs contain connection events, handshake status, audio engine status, GPS acquisition status, and error messages. Logs are stored locally and are never transmitted to us or any third party automatically.

ℹ️ No analytics, no crash SDK, no telemetry. Digi Voice does not integrate Firebase, Mixpanel, Sentry, Crashlytics, or any analytics framework. ALGSoft Inc. receives zero telemetry from your device.
Section 03

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to operate the application's core radio communication features:

  • 🔐
    Protocol Authentication
    Callsign, digital ID, ESSID, and password are included in protocol handshake packets sent to the server you select.
  • 📍
    Position Reporting
    Coordinates you enter or retrieve via GPS are included in protocol configuration packets. This is standard in DMR/BrandMeister/TGIF protocols.
  • 🔍
    Callsign / ID Lookup
    When you tap the ID lookup button, your callsign is sent to radioid.net via HTTPS to retrieve your registered DMR or NXDN ID.
  • 🎙️
    Voice Transmission
    Microphone audio is encoded and transmitted in real time to the radio server for the duration of PTT activation.

Section 05

Device Permissions

  • 🎙️
    Microphone — Required for PTT
    Requested on first PTT attempt. Audio is captured only while PTT is active and is transmitted exclusively to the radio server you are connected to. The app does not record audio to a file.
  • 📍
    Location (When In Use) — Optional
    Requested only when you tap "Get GPS Location." Uses requestWhenInUseAuthorization() — background location is never requested. Location service stops immediately after coordinates are retrieved.
  • 🌐
    Local Network — For local hotspot connections
    Required to allow UDP connections to DMR hotspot devices on your local Wi-Fi network. Does not grant access to files or data on other devices.
Section 06

Data Security

All settings, including passwords, are stored using UserDefaults on your local device, protected by iOS/macOS Data Protection and your device's passcode or biometrics.

Passwords are transmitted to network servers in the format required by the protocol: DMR uses a SHA-256 hash; other protocols may transmit within the UDP payload. We recommend using network-specific hotspot security keys rather than primary account credentials.

Section 07

Data Retention and Deletion

Settings: Stored indefinitely until you reset or delete the app. Delete Digi Voice from your device to remove all settings.

Log Files: Stored in the Caches directory. The OS may delete them under storage pressure. You can clear logs manually from the Logs tab. All logs are removed when the app is deleted.

ℹ️ We do not retain any data on our servers because we do not receive any data from your device in our infrastructure.
Section 08

Children's Privacy

Digi Voice is intended for licensed amateur radio operators and is not directed at persons under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used this application without appropriate supervision, please contact us.

Section 09

International Users

Digi Voice is developed in Türkiye. All data you enter is stored locally on your device. When you connect to radio network servers, your data is transmitted to servers operated by third parties in various countries. By using network features, you consent to this cross-border transmission as part of the radio network operation.

Section 10

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date and, where appropriate, provide notice within the application. Continued use of Digi Voice after the effective date of a revised policy constitutes your acceptance of the changes.

Bölüm 05

Cihaz İzinleri

  • 🎙️
    Mikrofon — PTT için zorunlu
    İlk PTT denemesinde talep edilir. Ses yalnızca PTT aktifken yakalanır ve yalnızca bağlı olduğunuz radyo sunucusuna iletilir. Uygulama sesi dosyaya kaydetmez.
  • 📍
    Konum (Kullanımdayken) — İsteğe bağlı
    "GPS Konumunu Al" düğmesine dokunulduğunda talep edilir. requestWhenInUseAuthorization() kullanılır — arka plan konumu hiçbir zaman talep edilmez. Koordinatlar alındıktan sonra konum servisi hemen durdurulur.
  • 🌐
    Yerel Ağ — Yerel hotspot bağlantıları için
    Yerel Wi-Fi'deki DMR hotspot cihazlarına UDP bağlantısı için gereklidir. Diğer cihazlardaki dosya veya verilere erişim sağlamaz.
